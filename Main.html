ASTRO WAR
With Astro War you will be able to fly through the space using your favorites power-ups, weapons, jetpacks, either bullets. Upgrade your items to beat the enemies or even other players!
Each item has a different function.
Play over 10 game modes, containing multiplayer, duo game mode.
Complete your daily missions to gain rewards!

Buy your favorites items in the AstroShop allowing you to get a different function for each upgrade of your weapons, jetpacks, bullets, shields.

Open chests to obtain new items and be able to get stronger.
The most valuable chest the best items you'll get!

Fight with different kind of enemies and face the big final boss at the end of a chapter!
